Execution Plan

30-Day POC Timeline

From zero to live campaigns, ad tracking, and ROI proof in four weeks.

Week 1 · Days 1–7

Data Foundation

Provision Data Cloud and configure the unified data model across all brands.

Ingest customer data from all 4 Marketo instances via Bulk API — contacts, leads, engagement history.

Create DMOs for Individual, ContactPointEmail, and begin ad spend data ingestion from Google/Meta via S3 connectors.

Identity resolution — configure rulesets so one traveler across multiple instances becomes one Unified Individual.

Week 2 · Days 8–14

Email & Flows Live

CMS email content — create branded email assets via Connect API (block-based, native Salesforce CMS).

Data Cloud segments — build waterfall segments for engagement tiers (Hot/Warm/Cold) on Unified Individual.

Segment-Triggered Flows — first campaign goes live. Flow checks consent via CommSubscription, resolves email via the identity chain, sends personalized content per tier.

Consent management — CommSubscription model configured for compliance.

Week 3 · Days 15–21

Ad Tracking & Omnichannel

Custom ad spend DMOs — campaign, channel, spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, revenue all modeled in Data Cloud.

Calculated Insights — ROAS, revenue per conversion, cost per acquisition built as reusable metrics with scheduled refresh.

Activation targets — export high-value audience segments to S3/GCS for ad platform syncing.

SMS + WhatsApp — native flow elements activated for booking confirmations and travel alerts.

Week 4 · Days 22–30

ROI Proven

ROI performance reports — spend vs. revenue by campaign, channel, and brand generated directly from Data Cloud.

Engagement scoring — Calculated Insights power engagement scores; high-score travelers auto-create CRM leads via flows.

Profile Insight widgets — CAC, ROAS, engagement score visible on Lead/Contact records in-context.

Migration playbook — documented path for consolidating remaining Marketo instances. Stakeholder presentation.

Steelman Assessment

Can This Actually Be Done in 30 Days?

An honest assessment accounting for human bottlenecks, campaign physics, and the reality that software doesn't run on its own.

Where Time Actually Goes

The individual task cards above show 90-100% automation rates within each task type. But those automatable tasks represent only ~55% of the total 30-day effort. The other 45% is time the team spends waiting — not building.

~55%
Automatable Build Work

Data ingestion, DMO creation, identity resolution, segment building, CMS content, flow construction, Calculated Insights, activation targets, report generation. All programmatic at 90-100% automation rates. Two FDEs in parallel compress these to days, not weeks.

~25%
Human Dependencies

Client data exports, brand assets, ad platform credentials, campaign content approval, dashboard UAT, final presentation. This is the critical path. FDEs are blocked during these windows.

~20%
Campaign Physics

Campaigns need 7 days to generate meaningful data. Emails need time for opens and clicks. Conversion attribution needs 7-day windows. You cannot compress physics — this time is non-negotiable.

Verdict: Yes, With Conditions

✓
Two FDEs make it feasible. FDE 1 (Data + Intelligence) and FDE 2 (Marketing Execution) work in parallel. While FDE 1 builds the data foundation in Week 1, FDE 2 prepares consent, content templates, and flow structures. No wasted time.
✓
MeshMesh compresses build work dramatically. What takes an SI 2-3 weeks (data model, segments, flows, content), MeshMesh executes in 2-3 days per workstream. The automation is real.
⚠
Client responsiveness is the #1 risk. If any UAT gate takes more than 48 hours, the 30-day timeline is in jeopardy. A dedicated Expedia reviewer with 4-6 hrs/week availability is a hard requirement.
⚠
ROI data will be directional, not definitive. With 7-10 days of campaign data, you get open rates, click rates, initial conversions, and early ROAS signals. Frame Day 30 as "early indicators" with a clear path to full measurement at Day 60.
✓
Pre-engagement prep is the biggest accelerator. If Expedia pre-stages Marketo exports, brand assets, and ad platform access during deal close, Week 1 is pure build — no waiting.

Risk Register

What Could Slow This Down

Transparent risk assessment with mitigations. Every risk here has a plan.

HIGH Client Data Delivery Delays

If Marketo exports arrive after Day 5, the entire Data Cloud track shifts right, cascading into MCA and MCI.

Mitigation: Pre-engagement data readiness checklist. Export templates provided in Week -1.

HIGH UAT Review Cycles > 48 Hours

If stakeholders take a week to review instead of 48 hours, Campaign 1 slips from Day 17 to Day 22+, compressing the data window.

Mitigation: Dedicated client reviewer assigned pre-engagement. Daily 15-min sync during UAT gates.

MEDIUM Identity Resolution Quality

If the 4 Marketo instances have inconsistent email formats or heavy duplication, match rates may be low, requiring re-tuning.

Mitigation: FDE 1 runs sample resolution on Day 5 with small dataset. Full run only after validation.

MEDIUM MCI Licensing Availability

MCI is a separate SKU. If not provisioned before Day 10, the entire MCI workstream is blocked.

Mitigation: Confirm license during deal close. Fallback: ROI reports from Data Cloud queries (no MCI).

LOW Campaign Volume Too Low

If POC email lists are under 5K contacts, engagement data may be statistically insignificant.

Mitigation: Target 10K+ contacts per campaign. Supplement with historical ad data for ROI baselines.

LOW Calculated Insight SQL Limits

Data Cloud CIs don't support DATEDIFF, CURRENT_DATE, or CAST. Rolling windows require numeric workarounds.

Mitigation: MeshMesh uses known workaround patterns. Core ROAS/CPA metrics fully achievable.

In Practice

How MeshMesh Works

An FDE or your own team tells MeshMesh what to build in plain language. MeshMesh executes the full operation on Marketing Cloud Next.

Tiered Welcome Campaign

"Create a welcome flow with waterfall segments — Hot travelers get a personalized destination email, Warm get deals, Cold get a brand intro"

MeshMesh creates CMS email content for each tier, builds the waterfall segment, creates a Segment-Triggered Flow with decision branches, configures consent, and publishes.

~20 min vs. days manually
Ad Spend ROI Analysis

"Show me ROAS by campaign for the last 30 days, broken down by channel"

MeshMesh queries Data Cloud ad spend DMOs, calculates revenue per conversion, ROAS, and CPA by campaign and channel. Generates a branded performance report with recommendations.

~5 min vs. hours of spreadsheets
Re-Engagement Segment

"Build a segment of high-value travelers who haven't booked in 30+ days and sync to Meta for retargeting"

MeshMesh creates a Calculated Insight for LTV, builds a segment on Unified Individual, publishes it, and exports to an S3 activation target for Meta audience sync. Auto-refreshes continuously.

~10 min vs. multi-day ops request
SMS + WhatsApp Booking Flow

"Set up an SMS and WhatsApp confirmation that fires when a booking event hits Data Cloud"

MeshMesh creates an Automation Event-Triggered Flow listening for booking events. Adds SMS and WhatsApp send elements, configures consent, and publishes. Native MC Next channels — no bolt-ons.

~15 min vs. hours of setup
